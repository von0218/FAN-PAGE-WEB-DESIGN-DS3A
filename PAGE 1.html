Debut Album

"SIS (Soft Is Strong)" is the title of the debut EP by the global girl group KATSEYE, released on August 16, 2024, under HYBE and Geffen Records. The EP's title embodies one of KATSEYE's core beliefs, "Soft Is Strong," and explores the sisterhood formed by its diverse members from different cultures. It features five tracks, including the pre-released singles "Debut" and "Touch".

Second Album

"Beautiful Chaos" is the second extended play (EP) by the K-pop group KATSEYE, released on June 27, 2025. The EP features a mix of Pop and R&B genres and includes triple title tracks: "Gnarly," "Gabriela," and "Gameboy". The album's theme celebrates the confusion and growth experienced during an artist's journey, expressing their artistic boundaries and embracing their evolving selves.
